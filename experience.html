Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur

During this tenure, I was engaged in a research role focused on Information Retrieval and Data Analysis. I conducted concurrent data collection processes to extract and analyze bug-related data from 280,000 Ubuntu bug pages hosted on Launchpad.

In addition, I implemented four distinct text distance algorithms. Using these algorithms I clustered bugs that exhibited similar keywords. This aided in the identification of trends, common issues and similarities among all of the bug related information

Furthermore, I constructed a comprehensive knowledge graph. with 47,000 bug nodes and 200,000 edges, which served as the relationship between bugs. Using this graph I derived valuable statistical insights, contributing to a deeper understanding of the underlying patterns.
